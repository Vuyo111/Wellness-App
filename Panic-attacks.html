Panic Attacks

What is it?

Panic attacks are sudden episodes of intense fear or discomfort that trigger physical reactionsâ€”often without an obvious cause.


Signs & Symptoms

  • Racing heartbeat
  • Sweating or shaking
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or dizziness
  • Fear of dying or losing control

Coping Tips

  • Focus on slow deep breathing
  • Remind yourself: "This will pass"
  • Splash cold water on your face
  • Ground yourself with sensory input (touch, sound)
  • Avoid caffiene or stimulants

When to Consult a Professional

  • You fear having another attcak
  • When panic attacks affect your ability to leave the house
  • You avoid situations due to fear
